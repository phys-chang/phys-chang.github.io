Research

My research focuses on theoretical aspects of non-Hermitian and open quantum many-body systems. I am interested in how topology, symmetry, dissipation, and interactions give rise to unconventional spectral and dynamical phenomena.

Recent topics include non-Bloch band theory, algebraic skin effects, Lindbladian dynamics, strong-to-weak symmetry breaking, and quantum many-body dynamics in dissipative systems.
